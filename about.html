About Us

Welcome to Humanti!

Humanti is an online platform built to spread awareness about the increasing effects of plastic usage, one of the most pressing environmental issues we face today. Our goal is inform, educate, and connect our users with the tools they need to get involved. By providing tips and resources, we hope we can encourage the switch to alternative forms of basic plastics. There are a bunch of tiny adjustments we can all make to our lives, that can contribute to the overall health of our planet. More than anything at all, Humanti's purpose is to give people the knowledge needed to stir up conversation and start talking about plastic pollution.
